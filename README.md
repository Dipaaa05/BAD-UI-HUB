# Bad UI Hub

Have you ever thought that changing your volume was too simple, boring, and lacking in thrills? 
**We fixed that for you.**

This project is a growing collection of mini-games (or rather, psychological tortures) designed with a single goal in mind: to make trivial actions — like turning up the volume — as frustrating, difficult, and irritating as possible.

Welcome to the ultimate hell of User Experience.

## The Collection: Volume Control Edition

Here is our current roster of digital suffering categories. Try to get your volume to 100% without losing your mind:

### Categories:

1. **Casinò**: Luck won't save you here.
2. **Daily Life**: Select your everyday nightmare.
3. **Phisycs**: Gravity and friction are your enemies.
4. **Sport**: Sweat and tears for a decent volume.
5. **Misc**: Fast paced UI disasters.

## How to Play (or how to drive your friends crazy)

No installation required. It's pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
Just clone the repository or download the files and open `index.html` in any modern browser. 

**[Click here to play the Live version right now!](https://bad-ui-hub.vercel.app/)**
